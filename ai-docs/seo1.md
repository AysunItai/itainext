Important style direction:
Keep the website elegant, smart, minimal, and premium.
Do not make it look like a cheap SEO landing page.
Do not keyword-stuff.
Do not add noisy sections, too many icons, or aggressive sales copy.
The tone should be calm, professional, confident, and human.

Business positioning:
ITAI Web Solutions helps small businesses, consultants, founders, and service providers build modern digital systems:

* professional websites
* booking flows
* WhatsApp contact flows
* SEO-ready pages
* dashboards
* internal tools
* practical AI automation

Goal:
Help Google understand the services more clearly while also helping visitors understand what I do within a few seconds.

Please work in this order:

1. Improve homepage keyword clarity

Keep the current visual design, but make sure the homepage includes a clear, natural sentence like:

“ITAI Web Solutions helps small businesses build modern websites, booking systems, WhatsApp contact flows, SEO-ready pages, dashboards, and practical AI automation.”

This should appear naturally in the hero intro or an early section.
Do not make it sound robotic.
Do not overload the hero with too much text.

Also make sure the homepage clearly communicates:

* who I help: small businesses, consultants, service providers, founders
* what I build: websites, booking flows, automations, dashboards
* why it matters: more leads, less manual work, better online presence

2. Add focused service pages

Please create elegant, SEO-friendly service pages for these core services:

/services/small-business-website-design
/services/ai-automation-for-small-business
/services/website-with-booking-system
/services/website-with-whatsapp-integration
/services/website-redesign
/services/seo-setup-for-small-business

* H1 with the service name
* short intro explaining who this service is for
* the business problem
* the solution I provide
* what is included
* who it is best for
* approximate process or timeline
* short FAQ section
* clear CTA to book a free consultation

“world-class solutions”
“revolutionary digital transformation”
